Mo:Mo Recipes

Momo is a popular Nepali dish, consisting of steamed dumplings filled with a variety of ingredients.

It's a favorite street food and comfort dish enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

###For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • water (as needed)
  • salt (a pinch)

###for the filling:

  • 250g ground meat (chicken,pork,or lamb)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger(minced)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it's smooth and pliable.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  4. In a pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and minced ginger. Saut√© until fragrant.
  6. Add the ground meat and cook until it's browned.
  7. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and remove from heat.
  8. Divide the dough into small equal-sized portions.
  9. Roll each portion into a thin, circular wrapper, about 3-4 inches in diameter.
  10. Place a spoonful of the meat filling in the center of each wrapper.
  11. To shape the momo, fold the wrapper in half over the filling to create a half-moon shape.
  12. Pinch and pleat the edges to seal the momo. You can choose different folding styles.
  13. Grease a steamer or line it with cabbage leaves to prevent sticking.
  14. Arrange the momo in the steamer, making sure they don't touch each other.
  15. Steam the momo for about 10-15 minutes, or until the wrappers are cooked and translucent.
  16. Serve the momo hot with a dipping sauce made from tomato chutney, sesame sauce, or soy-based sauce.
