Alu Tama Recipes

One of the delicious Nepali soups to cook is Alu Tama.

Those who love the combination of tanginess and spiciness would love Alu Tama.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bamboo shoots (tama), soaked and cleaned
  • 1 cup potatoes (alu), peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup black-eyed peas (bodi)
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 2-3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Chopped fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish

Recipes Instructions

  1. Soak bamboo shoots (tama) and black-eyed peas (bodi) (if using) in water.
  2. Peel and dice potatoes (alu).
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and dried red chilies.
  4. Saut√© garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  5. Add diced potatoes and soaked bamboo shoots.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili, cumin, coriander, fenugreek seeds, and salt.
  7. Cover and cook until potatoes and bamboo shoots are tender.
  8. Add soaked black-eyed peas and cook a bit more.
  9. Adjust seasoning and add a squeeze of lemon juice.
  10. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
  11. Enjoy Alu Tama with rice or roti.
